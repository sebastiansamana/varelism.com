## Concept

The design uses a sequence of compressed and expanded rooms around a planted courtyard, creating privacy without sacrificing openness.

## Material Strategy

Pale brick, brushed oak, and soft lime plaster were selected for tonal continuity. Large-format sliding panes dissolve thresholds between interior and landscape.

## Environmental Performance

A hybrid ventilation strategy, high thermal mass walls, and roof-mounted photovoltaics reduce annual energy demand while supporting long-term adaptability.
