## Program

The atelier combines artist studios, a workshop, and an adaptable gallery loop that supports day-to-day production and evening events.

## Spatial Character

The interiors are intentionally minimal: exposed timber trusses, polished concrete, and a restrained palette that foregrounds art and craft.

## Urban Response

A porous ground floor and landscaped forecourt connect the building to the riverside path, extending public life into the site.
