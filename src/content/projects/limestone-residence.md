## Site Logic

Built into a steep topography, the house follows the terrain in staggered platforms that minimize excavation and preserve existing stone walls.

## Interior Atmosphere

A sequence of calm spaces is defined by filtered light, deep reveals, and tactile materials selected to age gracefully over time.

## Construction

Prefabricated timber roof cassettes reduced site time, while local limestone cladding ties the project to regional building traditions.
